# 🏭 Industrial Safety NLP Chatbot

🚀 AI-powered chatbot to predict accident severity and safety risks from incident descriptions using NLP & ML/DL.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Analyze accident descriptions  
- Predict **Accident Level** & **Potential Risk**  
- Assist safety professionals with actionable insights  

---

## 📂 Dataset
- 418 records (after cleaning)  
- Data from **12 plants across 3 countries**  
- Includes accident details, risk type, and descriptions  

---

## 🧠 Approach
- NLP preprocessing: cleaning, tokenization, lemmatization  
- Feature engineering: encoding categorical variables  
- Built **14 models**:
  - 9 Machine Learning models  
  - 2 Neural Networks  
  - 3 LSTM models  

---

## 📊 Results
- 🏆 Best Model: **LSTM with SMOTE**  
- Accident Level Prediction Accuracy: **74%**  
- Potential Risk Prediction Accuracy: **48%**  

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
`Python` | `Scikit-learn` | `TensorFlow/Keras` | `NLTK` | `Pandas`

---

## 💬 Chatbot
- Input: Accident description  
- Output: Predicted severity + risk insights  

---

## ⚠️ Limitations
- Small dataset  
- Class imbalance
